Homemade Cheese Sticks

Ingredients
Hard cheese - 300 g
Egg - 1 pc.
Flour - 30 g
Vegetable oil - 70 ml

Steps
Prepare products for cheese sticks.
Cheese grate on a fine grater, add flour, egg.
Mix. Form cheese sticks.
Fry them in vegetable oil until golden brown.
Cheese sticks are ready!
Enjoy your meal!
