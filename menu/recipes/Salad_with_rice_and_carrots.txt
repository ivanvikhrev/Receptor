Salad with rice and carrots

Ingredients
100 grams of boiled rice
3 eggs
2 large carrots
2 medium bulbs
to taste Garlic
to taste Salt, pepper
Mayonnaise dressing

Steps
The rice was ready, put it in a salad bowl.
Cook and clean the eggs, skip through the egg cutter.
Peel the carrots and grate.
Peel the onions and chop finely chop.
Peel the garlic and pass through the press.
Salt, pepper and grease with mayonnaise.
Let stand. Salad ready.
