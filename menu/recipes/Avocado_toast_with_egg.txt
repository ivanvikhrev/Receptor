Avocado toast with egg

Ingredients
Whole wheat bread
1/2 avocado
1 egg
to taste Salt, pepper

Steps
We take a slice of bread, fry it in a dry frying pan.
Peel the avocados, pull out the pulp with a spoon and knead to a mash.
Put the pan on the fire, pour in some oil, 
break one egg and fry in a well heated pan. 
Add salt and pepper to taste. 
Cover with a lid, wait 3-5 minutes.
Putting our sandwich. First a toast, 
followed by avocado-mashed, followed by scrambled eggs.
