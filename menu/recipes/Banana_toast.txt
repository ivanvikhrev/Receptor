Banana topped with cinnamon

Ingredients
Bread
Banana
Cinnamon

Steps
Toast bread or loaf grease with softened butter.
For each piece, put a couple of slices of banana,
pour condensed milk,
Sprinkle with cane sugar and cinnamon.
Cover with another piece of bread
so that the butter is on top.
Send for 30 seconds in the microwave 
or toast in a waffle iron.
