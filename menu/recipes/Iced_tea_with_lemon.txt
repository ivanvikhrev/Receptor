Iced tea with honey, lemon and mint

Ingredients
2 liters of cold water
1 liter of hot water
1-2 tablespoons of tea
1 lemon
3 tablespoons honey
Mint bunch

Steps
Brew tea (any that you like: black or green) 
with a sprig of mint in a teapot.
Pour the tea into the jar, 
add hot water. Add honey. Stir to dissolve honey. 
Add mint. Squeeze out most of the lemon, pour it into the jar. 
Mix. Cut the rest of the lemon as you like (into slices or half rings) 
and add to the jar. Cool it down. 
Top up with cold water. Mix. Keep refrigerated. 
Serve with a slice of lemon and a sprig of mint. Enjoy!!!
