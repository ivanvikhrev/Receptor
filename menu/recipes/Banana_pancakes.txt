Banana Pancakes

Ingredients
2 ripe bananas
2 eggs
2 tablespoons of oatmeal
1 pinch of salt

Steps
Bananas peel and mash with a fork.
Add eggs and salt, mix with a whisk.
Pour the flour and mix again.
Bake in a dry pan without oil.
Served with sour cream and maple syrup 
or honey.
Decorate with fresh fruit.
