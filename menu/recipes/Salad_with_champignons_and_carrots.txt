Salad with champignons and raw carrots

Ingredients
300 g champignon
30 g cheese
1 onion
1 carrot
To taste salt, pepper, spices
Mayonnaise dressing
Oil for Roasting

Steps
Mushrooms washed and cut into plates.
Add butter to the pan and spread the mushrooms.
After 5 minutes add the chopped onion.
Fry until cooked.
At the end add salt, pepper, spices (I had nutmeg and ginger).
Carrot and cheese rubbed on a coarse grater.
Mix the ingredients and add mayonnaise (you can sour cream, natural yogurt).
